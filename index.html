Just as massive amounts of money are poured into presidential campaigns, massive amounts of money are poured into Hillary Clinton's campaign

like the candidates' own money

Why are these numbers so small?

These PACs aren't the same a Super PACs. Super PACs do spend large amounts of money on elections, but cannot give directly to candidates. Regular PACs, shown here, have donation restrictions because they can give directly to candidates
Clinton still has significantly more than all other candidates.

Contributions by employment status

This chart shows the breakdown of contributions by contributors of variuos employment statuses for each candidate.

Let's a take a closer look.
Across all candidates, most contributors are employed while half as many are retired and some are unemployed.
We can see that a very small percent of contributors to Republican candidates are unemployed.
A higher percent of contributors to Democratic candidates are unemployed.
Bernie Sanders has by far the highest percent of unemployed contributors.
